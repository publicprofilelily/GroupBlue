User stories:
As a customer, I want to be able to....

1. Order a cake with a theme that suits a particular occasion
2. Have it customized with personal decorations
3. Choose between different cake sizes (small, medium, large, extra large)
3. Have the cake delivered within 48 hours


Product specifications:
The product shall....

1. Look delicious
2. Have 2 or more layers based on sponge cake or chocolate cake
3. Have icing and decorations in accordance with a theme (such as chocolate, fruit or marzipan)
4. Be listed with a price on the web site
5. Be added to the shopping cart when the customer clicks the purchase button
6. Be removed from the shopping card if a customer clicks the remove button
